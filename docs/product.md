# Kento Product Spec

## Core user
Restaurant owner or operations manager.

## Main pains
- No clear visibility into inventory
- Waste and overportioning
- Poor food cost visibility
- High staff turnover
- Inconsistent execution of operational tasks

## MVP features

### 1. Inventory Intelligence
Users can create ingredients, units, stock levels, and supplier costs.
Users can log purchases, waste, adjustments, and usage.
Inventory should connect to recipes.

### 2. Menu & Recipes
Users can create menu items and define ingredient composition.
Each recipe determines stock deduction per sale.

### 3. Food Cost Engine
Menu items should automatically calculate cost from ingredient costs.
Show cost, selling price, margin, and food cost percentage.

### 4. Staff Operations
Users can create role-based checklists.
Employees can complete checklists.
Managers can review completion.

### 5. Dashboard
Summarize critical operational metrics:
- low stock alerts
- food cost average
- top profitable items
- checklist completion rate

## Non-goals for MVP
- marketing automation
- CRM
- customer loyalty
- reservations
- delivery management
- payroll
